Sit on Motorcycle
First do a Safety Check.
  1. Is your helmet on?
  2. Are you wearing long pants?
  3. Do you have closed toed shoes on?
  4. How are the weather conditions?
  5. Is the bike in working order?

When you first sit on a motorcycle it's a good idea to take a moment and think about your surroundings.
